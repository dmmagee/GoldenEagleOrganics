LAMB Products

GEO's Lamb

We highly enjoy raising our lamb on the farm, because they grow up with so much unique personality and comical sounds of laughter. Lamb, Hogget, and Mutton are meat names of domestic sheep at different ages. Younger Lamb are smaller and more tender. Mutton is meat from a sheep over two years old, and has less tender meat. In general, the darker the color of meat, the older the animal.

What We Do

In keeping with USDA Organic approved regulations, and our own personal caring touch around the farm, GEO strives to maintain a high standard of organic meats with competitive prices. Our lamb are 100% grass fed and given adequate shelter and fields to roam freely. It is our goal at GEO to earn your seal of approval with our livestock.
